    Three months with NixOS

    May 27, 2023

    In this article I’ll share with you pros and cons of NixOS system after using it for three months. This article will have another version released some time in the future so I can examine if deeper understanding of Nix will make using it easier and we’ll also watch how NixOS ecosystem is developping. First things first - my NixOS configuration is publicly available at my github, feel free to lurk around and see how I’m configuring my system. If you think that my configuration could be improved, don’t hesitate and leave comment. I’d like this repository to be something that others can use to bootstrap their configurations :-)
